Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly am I buying?

A Google Sheets template with built-in Apps Script automations. You get your own copy that connects to your Lofty CRM via Zapier. Tracks leads through a 6-stage pipeline, calculates GCI/ROI, and emails reports automatically.

Do I need Zapier paid plan?

No — the free Zapier tier supports up to 5 Zaps, but you'll need 6. Most teams use Zapier Starter ($19.99/month) or already have a plan. The ScoreCard OS sheet itself is $20/month.

What CRMs does it work with?

Currently Lofty CRM only. The Zaps are configured specifically for Lofty's data structure. More CRMs coming soon.

How long does setup take?

The onboarding call is 30 minutes. Setting up the 6 Zaps takes another 20 minutes. Most teams are fully live within 24 hours.

Can I customize the sheet?

Yes — it's your Google Sheet. Change colors, add columns, modify formulas. We provide documentation for common customizations.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

You keep the Google Sheet forever. It's yours. The automated reports and webhook stop, but all your data stays in your Drive.

How are the reports sent?

Apps Script sends emails directly from your Google account. You configure the sender and recipients in the Settings tab.

What if a deal falls through?

Change the status to Cancelled, Expired, or Fell Through. The deal stays in your records but is excluded from GCI calculations.
