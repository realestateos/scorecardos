Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Lofty CRM integration work?

We connect to your Lofty CRM via API. Once authorized, we automatically sync your lead data, appointments, and closing information. No manual exports or CSV uploads required.

We currently support Lofty, Follow Up Boss, KVCore, HubSpot, and Salesforce. We're adding new integrations monthly based on customer requests.

Data syncs every 4 hours automatically. You can also trigger a manual sync anytime from your dashboard. Weekly reports are generated every Monday morning.
